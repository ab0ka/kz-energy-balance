\chapter{Consolidated Summary Infographic}
\label{app:infographic}

A consolidated visual summary of the main quantitative findings of the
dissertation is presented across Figures~\ref{fig:summary_kpi}--\ref{fig:summary_findings}.
For readability the summary is divided into four panels: headline indicators
and the 2024 generation structure (Figure~\ref{fig:summary_kpi}), the
coal-to-gas fuel-switching dynamics (Figure~\ref{fig:summary_fuelshift}), the
regional and target context (Figure~\ref{fig:summary_context}), and the
consolidated key findings with policy implications
(Figure~\ref{fig:summary_findings}).
